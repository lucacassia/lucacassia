About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage! I am a research fellow at the University of Melbourne with a strong passion for mathematical and theoretical physics.

I earned my PhD in theoretical physics from the University of Milano-Bicocca in 2019. Subsequently, I held PostDoc positions at Uppsala University and Durham University. Currently, I am a PostDoc researcher in the mathematics department at the University of Melbourne.

Research Interests

My research interests include...

  • matrix models
  • topological recursion
  • BPS/CFT correspondence
  • quantum algebras
  • topological string theory
  • quantum cohomology and mirror symmetry
  • enumerative geometry
